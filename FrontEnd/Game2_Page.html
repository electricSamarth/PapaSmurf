Roller Madness -Ueagle490

Roller Madness is a Third person Adventure game, made by user Ueagle490.
Instructions:

  • W,A,S,D to move around.
  • Collect coins to Progress.
  • Black boxes Kill you so evade them.
  • Falling off the platform Leads to your death.
